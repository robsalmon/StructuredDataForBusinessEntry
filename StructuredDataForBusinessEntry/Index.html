MOT testing

We are able to carry out MOT testing of cars and small vans (up to 3.5 tonnes gross weight).

How to book

You should book your MOT online using the MOT booking calendar.

Availability

You can book tests to be carried out Monday to Friday 8am to 4pm (by appointment only). Each MOT takes approximately 1 hour.

How to pay

Once booking is complete payment can be made via the link below, or on the day of the booking by cash or card at the Transport reception.
